# Analytics Stage
The Analytics Stage is responsible providing observability and response features.
## Groups[](https://handbook.gitlab.com/handbook/engineering/data-engineering/analytics/#groups)
This stage consists of the following group:
  * [Analytics Instrumentation](https://handbook.gitlab.com/handbook/engineering/data-engineering/analytics/analytics-instrumentation/)
  * [Optimize](https://handbook.gitlab.com/handbook/engineering/data-engineering/analytics/optimize/)
  * [Platform Insights](https://handbook.gitlab.com/handbook/engineering/data-engineering/analytics/platform-insights/)


## Product Categories[](https://handbook.gitlab.com/handbook/engineering/data-engineering/analytics/#product-categories)
These groups map to the [Analytics Stage product category](https://handbook.gitlab.com/handbook/product/categories/).
## Demo Environments[](https://handbook.gitlab.com/handbook/engineering/data-engineering/analytics/#demo-environments)
In order to make it more efficient to verify changes and demonstrate our product features to customers and other stakeholders. The engineers in this stage maintain a few demo environments.
