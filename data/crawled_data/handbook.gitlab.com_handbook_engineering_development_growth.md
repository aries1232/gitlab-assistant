# Growth Stage
The Growth Stage consists of development teams working in the product delivering enhancements and running experiments
## Vision[](https://handbook.gitlab.com/handbook/engineering/development/growth/#vision)
Grow an agile fullstack development capability to support our product goals through feature development and experiments enabling GitLab to make informed product decisions and achieve results in line with our [product direction](https://handbook.gitlab.com/handbook/marketing/growth/).
For more details on Growth’s mission, direction, and product strategy, see the [Growth product handbook](https://handbook.gitlab.com/handbook/marketing/growth/).
## How We Work[](https://handbook.gitlab.com/handbook/engineering/development/growth/#how-we-work)
Working inline with our [values](https://handbook.gitlab.com/handbook/values/), we focus on [iteration](https://handbook.gitlab.com/handbook/values/#iteration) and [collaboration](https://handbook.gitlab.com/handbook/values/#collaboration), working with and across areas of the product our development department counterparts maintain.
We work on the issues prioritized by our product teams including running [experiments](https://handbook.gitlab.com/handbook/engineering/development/growth/experimentation/) on GitLab.com. Growth stage teams have Fullstack Engineers. The reason for this is that the Growth stage has a need for both Frontend and Backend skill-sets, but as a small team, has optimized for team member efficiency to adopt the Fullstack role.
