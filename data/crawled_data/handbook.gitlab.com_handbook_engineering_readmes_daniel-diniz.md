## About me[](https://handbook.gitlab.com/handbook/engineering/readmes/daniel-diniz/#about-me)
I’m passionate about life itself. I love learning new things, preferably on a daily basis. I like it so much that if I would have to name one goal in life, it would be to become a full-time learner.
  * Whenever I’m not behind a computer, I try to find any excuse to be outdoors, living life dynamically in many different ways. I like 🌴 [Slacklining](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Slacklining), 🏄 [Kitesurfing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kiteboarding), 🐶 🏂 [Dogsurfing](https://vimeo.com/258229614), 🗻 [Climbing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Climbing). I’m an adept of the 🐒 [Movement Culture](https://www.idoportal.com/).
  * I have many 🎵 musical instruments, and try to find time in between all other activities to explore musicality as much as I can.
  * My most recent passion is Carpentry 🔨; I really feel like human beings are meant to construct their own structures. I love setting out time to plan, design and build practical things, from chairs, tables and shelfs all the way to small houses and shelers.
  * Here are a couple of things I like doing _every day_ :
    * Going for a walk 🏃
    * Writing 📙
    * Spending time alone in silence 💭
    * At least one deep, meaningful conversation with someone I love 💬
    * Some deep, focused work :godmode:
    * A good night’s sleep 💤
